Selected Projects

ACL 2026 · Research

WIGVO: Real-Time Speech Translation over Legacy PSTN Calls

Real-time bidirectional speech translation running on the public telephone network (G.711 μ-law, 8 kHz). The Dual-Session Echo Gating architecture and a 3-stage audio filter pipeline remove the echo loop in software: zero echo loops across 147 completed real calls.
